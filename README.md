## Overview

**Indigenous Truth** transforms a six-part video series on Indigenous-colonial relations into a deeply researched, fully cited, visually immersive educational resource. Each video is broken down into 12+ major themes, and each theme expands into its own dedicated research page with primary sources, scholarly citations, legal documents, and historical context.

This is not a summary. It's a comprehensive archive.
